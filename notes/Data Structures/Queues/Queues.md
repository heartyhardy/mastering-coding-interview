#Queues

##Why Queues

* Great when you want to Know the item which was inserted first.
* **FIFO** First-In-First-Out
* Ticket counters, Uber rides and Restaurent orders are good examples of Queues

##Operations

| Operation     | O(n)   |
|:--------------|--------|
| Lookup        | `O(n`) |
| Enqueue       | `O(1)` |
| Dequeue       | `O(1)` |
| Peek          | `O(1)` |
