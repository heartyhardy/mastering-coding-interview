#Stacks

##Why Stacks
* Great when you want to know the last item inserted
* **LIFO** Last in First Out
* Used in many ways such as **Browser back** and **Undo**
  
##Operations

| Operation | O(n) |
|:----------|------|
| Lookup    | `O(n`) |
| Push      | `O(1)` |
| Pop       | `O(1)` |
| Peek      | `O(1)` |
